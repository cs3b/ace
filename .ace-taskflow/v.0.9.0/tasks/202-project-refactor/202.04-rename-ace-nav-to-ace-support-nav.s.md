# Rename ace-nav to ace-support-nav

## Objective

Rename the `ace-nav` gem to `ace-support-nav` to follow the established `ace-support-*` naming pattern. Keep the executable as `ace-nav` for backwards compatibility.

## Scope of Work

### Directory Rename
- `ace-nav/` ŌåÆ `ace-support-nav/`

### Namespace Change
- `Ace::Nav` ŌåÆ `Ace::Support::Nav`
- Version bump: 0.16.1 ŌåÆ 1.0.0 (major due to breaking namespace change)

### Executable
- Keep as `ace-nav` (no change to exe name)

### lib/ Structure Change
```
ace-nav/lib/ace/nav.rb       ŌåÆ ace-support-nav/lib/ace/support/nav.rb
ace-nav/lib/ace/nav/         ŌåÆ ace-support-nav/lib/ace/support/nav/
  version.rb                   version.rb
  atoms/                       atoms/
  molecules/                   molecules/
  organisms/                   organisms/
  models/                      models/
  cli.rb                       cli.rb
  commands/                    commands/
```

### Deliverables

#### Rename (directory)

- `ace-nav/` ŌåÆ `ace-support-nav/`

#### Modify (2 dependent gemspecs)


Gems to update:

- ace-review/ace-review.gemspec

Key files:

All usages of:

#### Modify (Gemfile)

