## Objective

Rename `ace-support-timestamp` to `ace-b36ts` (Base36 Timestamps) to better reflect the package's purpose and align with the `ace-*` naming convention for standalone CLI tools. The current name "timestamp" is generic and doesn't convey the core value: compact, chronologically-sortable Base36 IDs. The new name `b36ts` is distinctive and descriptive. Per ADR-024, no backward compatibility shims are needed pre-1.0. Follows the precedent of task 264 (ace-coworker â†’ ace-assign) and task 266 (ace-support-tmux â†’ ace-tmux).
