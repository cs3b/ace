# ace-models: migrate to standard help pattern

## Scope

Migrate ace-models from DefaultRouting to standard multi-command help pattern.

**Binary:** `ace-models`
**CLI module:** `ace-support-models/lib/ace/support/models/cli.rb`
**Pattern:** multi-command (registry)
**Current commands:** search, info, cost, sync, status, diff, clear
**Current routing:** DefaultRouting — clean delete, no custom logic
