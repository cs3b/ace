# Create Research Workflow with Multi-Agent Option

## Behavioral Specification

### User Experience
- **Input**: User invokes `/ace:research [topic]` or `ace-bundle wfi://research`
- **Process**: Workflow includes multi-agent research option
- **Output**: Single-agent research OR handoff to parallel-research

### Expected Behavior

The research workflow provides a standard single-agent research process with an explicit option to use multi-agent research when appropriate. It includes decision criteria for when to escalate to multi-agent approach and references the multi-agent research guide.

- [ ] Workflow created at `ace-handbook/handbook/workflow-instructions/research.wf.md`
